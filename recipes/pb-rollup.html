Peanut Butter Banana Rollup

Description

Staple household ingredients come together to make a delicious treat for hungry toddlers and parents alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 tortilla
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 banana

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on the tortilla
  2. Peel the banana and place it on the peanut butter tortilla
  3. Roll the banana up into the tortilla
  4. Cut into 1 inch slices
